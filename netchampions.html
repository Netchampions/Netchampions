About Us

NetChampions is a premier Pickleball Academy and Tournament Organization based in Chennai. We focus on training young and aspiring players, conducting school programs, and hosting state and national level tournaments. Our mission is to grow the sport and build a vibrant pickleball community across India.

Programs Offered

  • Structured Coaching for Kids and Adults
  • Skill-Based Group Sessions
  • Weekend & Holiday Pickleball Camps
  • School Demo & Integration Programs
  • Tournament Preparation & Mentoring

Our Events & Activities

  • Monthly Intra-Academy Tournaments
  • Open Championship Events
  • Pickleball Clinics and Exhibitions
  • Community Outreach & Demo Days
